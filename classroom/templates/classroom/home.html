What's this about?

Round 1 is a preliminary round which will separate the top teams & eliminate the rest.

The rules and regulations for round 1 are as follows:

  1. A team would comprise of 2 members.
  2. Registered teams will be assigned a dedicated computer to undertake quiz on a portal.
  3. The quiz comprises of the logical, technical and non-technical questions.
  4. The marking scheme is as follows:
    • A team can attempt or choose to skip a question.
    • For all successive correct attempts marks would be awarded as +2i, where i is the number of questions answered correctly. (Eg. +2,+4,+8â€¦so on)
    • Any wrong attempt/skipping the question will break the streak.
    • Similarly for all successive wrong attempts marks would be deducted as -2i.
    • There is no penalty for skipping the question.
  5. The time allotted for this event is 30-40 min.
